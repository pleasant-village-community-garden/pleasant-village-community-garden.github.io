Garden Rules

No pets, except for certified service dogs

Throw away your trash

No smoking in the garden

No alcohol in the garden

Taking produce from plots is prohibited; Please ask a garden member if you would like a piece of fruit from the trees.

Eggs are 3 for $1, or 6 for $2

Do not pick the flowers

A member should be present while visiting the garden. If a member needs to leave the garden, they have the right to ask you to leave so the garden gate can be locked for security purposes.
